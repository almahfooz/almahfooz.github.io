This introductory course provides a foundational understanding of data analytics, equipping 
participants with the essential skills to extract meaningful insights from raw data. The curriculum 
covers key concepts such as data collection, cleaning, transformation, and visualization. 
Participants will learn to utilize various statistical methods, exploratory data analysis 
techniques, and data mining algorithms to identify patterns, trends, and anomalies within
 datasets. The course will also introduce commonly used data analytics tools and software,
 enabling participants to apply their knowledge to real-world scenarios and make data-driven 
 decisions. By the end of the course, participants will have the necessary skills to effectively 
 analyze data, communicate findings, and contribute to data-driven decision-making processes 
 within their respective fields..
