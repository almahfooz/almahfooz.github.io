I am Mahfooz Ahmed, a dedicated and accomplished professional with a Doctorate in Library and Information Science (Open Science) from the International Islamic University Malaysia, with a strong foundation in open science, data and information science, and records and knowledge management.  Published in peer-reviewed journals, I am committed to advancing research in these fields.

I am proficient in multiple languages, including Arabic, English, Hausa, and Yoruba, and I possess advanced data analysis, programming, digital literacy, and cloud computing skills.

My background spans academia and professional practice, including roles as a Research Assistant, IT Assistant, and Library Assistant. My leadership roles and community service engagements, such as serving as President of the Nigerian Student Community in Malaysia, showcase my ability to lead and collaborate effectively.

My research interests include applying AI and spatial data science to address challenges in health and medical information databases, open science initiatives, and knowledge management. I am eager to continue contributing to these fields through innovative research and collaboration. In Shaa Allah!
